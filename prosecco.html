Valdobbiadene Superiore D.O.C.G

Prosecco Superiore Follador is produced with Glera grapes harvested exclusively by hand in the vineyards located in the heart of Conegliano Valdobbiadene, rigorously selected, crushed and cryomacerated according to the Gianfranco Follador® Method. This line, which represents our tribute to the excellence of a unique territory, includes five D.O.C.G .: the Superiore di Cartizze and five vintage wines in the "Torri di Credazzo", Extra-Dry, Brut, and Extra Brut Cru versions.

Prosecco DOC Treviso

The sparkling wines of the Follador D.O.C. Line are the expression of vineyards that produce high quality grapes. The vinification is carried out according to the Gianfranco Follador® Method, developed starting from a great heritage of traditions and family skills. All the grapes used come from hills located in the Conegliano Valdobbiadene, historic Prosecco Territory.

A Totally Pink Line

Rosé

An all pink line, with a fruity bouquet and hints of fresh flowers. For the most delicate palates, lovers of the typical freshness of Rosé prosecco. Three extremely fresh proposals, with many pink shades. This type of wine is extremely versatile and will be able to please your guests at any time of the meal.

The Native sparkling Wines Of The Territory

Spumanti Millesimati

Historically they represent the native sparkling wines of the Territory. They are mainly produced with grapes from vineyards located in the plains within the Prosecco area. After the cold maceration they are refermented in autoclave for about 60 days, obtaining fresh and fragrant wines, suitable for all convivial occasions.

About Follodor

The Follador Family cares a lot about its cultural heritage in the production of wine. It has neither stood still in the past, nor is it intimidated by modern times, but has quietly married traditional cultivation techniques in the vineyards with the most modern production technologies, this to obtain wines highly regarded even by experts in the sector.
