Follador Prosecco
Tasting & Visit

We will tell you a story with deep roots, dating back over 2 centuries ago. We will retrace the steps of our ancestors, up to the innovations of the present that keep traditions alive with a watchful eye towards the future.

The experience includes a welcome moment at the company, the viewing of a short film about the history of the company, a visit to the factory with an explanation of the production process. Finally, the guided tasting of 5 Valdobbiadene Prosecco Superiore is included.
