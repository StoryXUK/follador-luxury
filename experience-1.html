Follodor Prosecco
Prestige Perlage

Let us guide you to discover the very fine perlage of our Valdobbiadene Prosecco Superiore DOCG. An ancient history, made up of sacrifices and strong values, told by the quality of these fine wines and by a naming that evokes old family traditions.

The experience includes a welcome by the hospitality manager or by a family member in the main room followed by a the tasting of our 5 Valdobbiadene Prosecco Superiore DOCG.
