FOLLADOR PROSECCO AT THE FOREFRONT OF TENNIS

The renowned company is for the 5th consecutive year partner of the antico Tiro al Volo Club for the prestigious ATV Tennis Open WTA125

From July 13th to 20th 2025, on the occasion of the 15th edition of the ATV Tennis Open WTA125, Follador Prosecco dal 1769 will once again serve as the technical sponsor of the prestigious international women's tournament hosted by the Antico Tiro a Volo Club in Rome. Throughout the event, the historic Veneto-based winery will host a dedicated aperitif corner, offering guests and athletes the refined selection of its Prosecco Superiore DOCG.

The Follador corner, located in one of the Club’s convivial areas, will provide spectators and tournament participants with moments of relaxation and socialization, accompanied by the brand’s elegant sparkling wines. Highlighting the brand's presence will be the gala evening on July 16th, a special event within the tournament itself, dedicated to the athletes, sponsors, numerous guests and institutional representatives.

This event represents a significant occasion where the values of sport and competitive spirit blend with social commitment, creating a week dedicated to high-level tennis. Now firmly established among the elite of Italian sporting competitions, the tournament continues to captivate and engage a wide audience.

“Being a sponsor for five consecutive years of such an important and prestigious event is a great source of pride for us and a testament to the quality that has always distinguished our company. Our Prosecco Superiore tells a story of elegance, passion, and terroir, perfectly matching the prestige and values of this wonderful sport and this prestigious tournament” says Cristina Follador, Sales & Marketing Manager of Follador.

The Follador sparkling wine range stands out for the finesse of its bubbles, the result of the exclusive Gianfranco Follador® Method, a unique winemaking protocol that enhances the characteristics of the Glera grapes grown on the UNESCO heritage hills of Conegliano Valdobbiadene. These products serve as ambassadors of Italian excellence worldwide, perfectly suited to such an exclusive, exciting, and renowned setting as the ATV Tennis Open.
