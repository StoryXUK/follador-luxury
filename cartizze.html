Cartizze

Valdobbiadene Superiore D.O.C.G. Dry
Order

Cartizze is one of the most treasured expressions of Valdobbiadene, a wine that brings together elegance, softness, and unmistakable charm in every glass.

Crafted from 100% Glera grapes grown on clay and limestone soils in the heart of the Valdobbiadene D.O.C.G. area, this is a Prosecco Superiore shaped by a remarkable landscape, meticulous vineyard care, and generations of winemaking tradition.

Bright straw yellow with delicate green reflections, Cartizze reveals a fine and persistent perlage, an elegant floral bouquet, and a generous palate lifted by notes of apple, pear, and peach. Smooth, graceful, and beautifully composed, it is a wine created to satisfy even the most discerning palate.
