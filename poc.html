Axis 3 Action 3.4.1

Internationalization of Conegliano
Valdobbiadene Prosecco DOCG wine

The Veneto Region, with Regional Council Resolution (D.G.R.) no. 1949 dated 23.12.2015, recognized the Consortium for the Protection of Conegliano Valdobbiadene Prosecco Wine as the legal entity representing the industrial district of Conegliano Valdobbiadene Prosecco.

Follador Prosecco
The Estate

In this capacity, the Consortium takes on the role of project leader, with the following companies participating: ADAMI S.R.L., BIANCAVIGNA S.S. AGRICULTURAL COMPANY, CARPENE' MALVOLTI SPUMANTI S.P.A., FOLLADOR DI ROSSI ITALIA & C. S.N.C. WINE COMPANY, LE COLTURE DI RUGGERI CESARE & C. S.S. AGRICULTURAL COMPANY, VAL D'OCA S.R.L. List of planned activities:

  1. Design and participation in international trade fairs: Vinitaly 2022, Vinitaly 2023, ProWein 2022, and ProWein 2023
  2. Planning and organization of incoming activities for operators from target markets.

The objectives to be achieved through the aforementioned activities are:

  1. Increase the capacity for commercial openness through the diversification of the market outlets for companies in the Industrial District and, consequently, the regional production system.
  2. Strengthen the vocation for export and the level of internationalization of SMEs in the District participating in the project.
  3. Consolidate relationships related to the development of export activities within the "district system" and create opportunities for meeting supply and demand.
  4. Promote and enhance the image of the wines of participating companies among industry operators in the target markets, to strengthen awareness and increase the recognizability of denomination products, as well as increase the systemic competitiveness of the industrial district.
  5. Total public support granted: €216,187.70

About Follodor

The Follador Family cares a lot about its cultural heritage in the production of wine. It has neither stood still in the past, nor is it intimidated by modern times, but has quietly married traditional cultivation techniques in the vineyards with the most modern production technologies, this to obtain wines highly regarded even by experts in the sector.
