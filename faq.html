FAQ's

  • What is shop.folladorprosecco.com?
    It is an online shop where you can purchase Prosecco D.O.C.G., Prosecco D.O.C., sparkling wines, wines and accessories directly from the producer, Azienda Follador, located in the heart of Conegliano Valdobbiadene. Every product is accompanied by a comprehensive technical data sheet with suggestions and useful information to help customers choose.
  • Why should I choose shop.folladorprosecco.com?
    We provide a pleasant, intuitive shopping experience, products of the highest quality and a service that guarantees total customer satisfaction. Buying is quick, simple and safe.
  • How much does it cost and how do I sign up?
    Registration is easy and free of charge: click on "REGISTER" at the top of the page and follow the instructions. You will receive an email containing a link you must click on to confirm your email address. Registration gives you access to offers and you can purchase all the products in the catalogue. We recommend you subscribe to our newsletter to receive the latest news and special promotions. You can make purchases without being registered however: in this case just place the products in the cart and continue with your order by clicking on “continue as guest”.
  • How do I purchase products?
    All the products in the shop can be purchased directly online. Just select the product you want, add it to your cart and follow the purchase process until you have completed your order.

  • Will my bottles be safe during shipping?
    Orders are shipped by an express courier (TNT Italia). Our packaging consists of a double cardboard box which has been approved by the leading Italian and international couriers.
  • Which forms of payment do you accept?
    You can pay by credit card and prepaid cards belonging to the Visa, MasterCard, PostePay, Aura and PayPal card circuits and with a PayPal account.
  • Is it safe to pay by credit card?
    Paying by credit card on shop.folladorprosecco.com is safe and certified because the confidential data are not processed by our servers or our staff, but by the banking system only. They are not stored in our database or that of the bank. The transaction takes place using a secure, encrypted connection.
  • Are the prices on the website inclusive of VAT?
    All prices are inclusive of VAT.
  • How much are the shipping costs?
    The shipping costs are calculated according to the order total. For orders under € 85.00, the customer pays € 11.90 in Italy, islands excluded. For orders above € 85.00, shipping is free.
  • Are my personal data safe?
    We use a certified SSL with the highest level of protection currently available for web browsing which is extended to all the pages of the website (128/256-bit Extended Validation SSL Certificate issued by Let's Encrypt Authority X3). In this way your personal data are always encrypted. You can check the authenticity of the security certificate of shop.folladorprosecco.com in the relevant protection section of the browser you use which is normally next to the address bar, on the page title or on the status bar. Information regarding payment, like the credit card details, do not pass through and are not processed/stored by shop.folladorprosecco.com because they are processed directly by the banking system. Payment by PayPal ensures the consumer maximum protection and confidentiality.
  • I’ve forgotten my password, what should I do?
    To recover your password, just click on "ACCESS" and select “If you can’t remember your password, click here to recover it” in the Password field. Once you have entered your email address and clicked on the relative link, you will receive an email message with your access credentials.
