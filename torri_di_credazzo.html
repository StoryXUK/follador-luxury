Torri di Credazzo Cru

Valdobbiadene Prosecco Superiore Millesimato DOCG Extra Dry
Torri di Credazzo Cru
Order

In the heart of the terroir, these austere Medieval towers look out over the Follador family vineyards. The long Charmat process offers consistent quality and brings pleasure to even the most demanding palates. Bright straw yellow color with greenish reflections. Fine perlage, elegant bouquet with hints of apple, peach and rose petals. Great as aperitif and with pasta dishes based on fish and shellfish.

Organoleptic
Profile

Colour

Bright straw yellow with lime green reflections. Fine, persistent bead.

Bouquet

Elegant on the nose with hints of apple, peach and rose petals.

Flavour

Soft but full-bodied, sapid and satisfying.

Pairings

Perfect as an aperitif and with fish and shellfish first courses.
