Internationally Renowned
Follador Worldwide

The Follador Company proudly represents the excellence of Prosecco DOCG around the world, also through a careful distribution policy aimed at the most demanding and cosmopolitan consumers. The Italian lifestyle has always exerted an indisputable charm abroad, where it is perceived as an effective status symbol: this is why we have become suppliers of some of the most prestigious hotels in the world. We are proud to represent the uniqueness and value of our inimitable Territory in these prestigious contexts: Conegliano Valdobbiadene.

London
The Dorchester

The Dorchester hotel is a luxurious 5-star hotel located in the heart of London, east of Hyde Park. A historic place, where you can indulge in the pleasures of a chic and elegant stay, with every comfort available. It opened its doors for the first time in 1931 and, despite numerous renovations, it still retains much of its original high-class furnishings.

It has always hosted prominent personalities, such as famous writers, painters or international show business personalities. Its solemn atmosphere has allowed it to host important literary gatherings over the years.

Rome
Marriot Grand Hotel Flora

A luxurious neoclassical building, nestled by Villa Borghese park in Rome, is a cosmopolitan haven for refined luxury enthusiasts. Its timeless elegance makes it an ideal base for both leisure and business travelers, offering convenient access to key attractions. The hotel boasts a well-equipped business area, catering to the needs of demanding tourists and business meetings alike. For a chic getaway, this is a truly classy option. And of course, Follador Prosecco graces our majestic capital.

Venice
Hotel Cipriani

For a Glamor stay in the pearl of Northern Italy, this property is a daydream. Located on the Giudecca island, away from the tourist crowds, easily reachable by private motorboat from the beautiful Piazza San Marco.

Guests will find themselves surrounded by large bright spaces, fine furnishings and original works of art, they will be fascinated by every window they look out on, enjoying a unique view that leaves little room for words.

Bangkok
Mandarin Oriental

Gianfranco has been able to carry on with great passion and excellence, the priceless values of his ancestors. He has developed a revolutionary method in the world of sparkling wine, deposited to protect his exclusivity. The Gianfranco Follador Method™, which bears his name, involves precise and well-studied steps that give the final product an incomparable quality, appreciated even by the most demanding connoisseurs. An innovative and highly performing system that allows you to obtain sparkling wines of great character.

Nepal
Dwarika's Resort - Dhulikhel

A place of peace and well-being, where the cornerstones of Buddhism make their way into the folds of the soul, instilling awareness and serenity. The deep roots of the Himalayan culture give each guest a stay of relaxation, meditation and unspoiled nature. Then there are our prestigious bubbles, for a lively pampering at the end of a regenerating massage or a relaxing Yoga lesson.

About Follodor

The Follador Family cares a lot about its cultural heritage in the production of wine. It has neither stood still in the past, nor is it intimidated by modern times, but has quietly married traditional cultivation techniques in the vineyards with the most modern production technologies, this to obtain wines highly regarded even by experts in the sector.
