Follador Prosecco invites you to Vinitaly 2026

From 12 to 15 April 2026, on the occasion of Vinitaly 2026, we will be delighted to welcome you to Verona, at Hall 5 – Stand D4, to share an experience devoted to elegance, a true expression of terroir, and the quality that has always defined Follador Prosecco.

For us, Vinitaly is far more than an international wine fair: it is a privileged moment for meeting, exchanging ideas and creating meaningful connections. It is also a special opportunity to introduce you to our vision of Prosecco Superiore DOCG — shaped by a deep respect for tradition, a commitment to excellence, and a contemporary, forward-looking approach.

Throughout the event, we will be pleased to guide you through a tasting of the new vintage of our Superior D.O.C.G. wines, a genuine expression of our land, and to unveil a new Prosecco collection, presented exclusively during Vinitaly.

It will also be a pleasure to share with you the values that inspire our work every day: quality, innovation and sustainability, in a journey where heritage and modern sensibility come together in perfect harmony.

Should you wish to enjoy a more dedicated experience during the fair, we invite you to schedule a meeting in advance. We would be pleased to welcome you personally and raise a glass together to this new edition of Vinitaly.

Vinitaly 2026
12–15 April 2026
Verona, Hall 5 – Stand D4

We look forward to welcoming you to Verona and to sharing the most authentic and elegant expression of Follador Prosecco.
