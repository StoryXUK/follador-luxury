The Follador Estate
A History Of Excellence

The winery located in Col San Martino covers an area of 8 hectares, divided into three areas: fermentation, bottling, and cellars. The entire process of production, from harvest to bottling, is done on the property.

Due to increasing interest in our wines, we’ve created a tasting room to welcome visitors. The elegant environment welcomes wine enthusiasts along the “Route of the Prosecco”. For special events and guided tours we serve excellent traditional food to accompany our wines.

The Region

Nestled in the romantic Veneto region, near historic Venice, lies the ideal Prosecco "Terroir." Col San Martino, at the heart of this Prosecco haven, offers superb wine-growing conditions and Follador Vineyards flourish in this perfect blend of micro-climate, regional support, and optimal soil composition.
