Follador Prosecco A Vinexpo Paris 2025

Join Follador Prosecco at Vinitaly 2025!

We are delighted to announce that Follador Prosecco will once again be attending Vinitaly 2025, the premier international wine and spirits exhibition.

From April 6th to 9th, we’ll be in Verona, Hall 5 - Stand D3, ready to share our passion, our wines, and the rich heritage of our family’s winemaking tradition.

A Celebration of Wine and Connection

Vinitaly is more than just an event—it’s a moment to connect, exchange ideas, and celebrate the artistry of winemaking with partners, friends, and professionals from across the globe.

Whether you're a wine enthusiast, a distributor, or a hospitality professional, we invite you to explore our exquisite Prosecco Superiore DOCG and experience the dedication behind every bottle.

Discover the Essence of Follador Prosecco

At our stand, you’ll have the opportunity to:

  • - Taste our award-winning Prosecco, crafted with precision and care
  • - Learn about our winemaking philosophy, rooted in over 250 years of tradition
  • - Engage with our team, who are passionate about quality, sustainability, and innovation

Let’s Connect

If you’d like to schedule a meeting during the event, we’d love to hear from you. Reach out in advance, and we’ll arrange a time to chat over a glass of Prosecco.

  • - Dates: April 6-9, 2025
  • - Location: Verona, Hall 5 - Stand D3

We can’t wait to welcome you to Vinitaly 2025 and share the true essence of Follador Prosecco. See you in Verona!
