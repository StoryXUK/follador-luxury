Azzura

Prosecco DOC Treviso Dry
Azzura Prosecco DOC
Order

Azzura: A memorable, sophisticated sparkling wine whose name reminds us of a cool, bright and clear blue sky.

A noble spumante. This wine is the perfect expression of the typical aromas and rich olfactory sensations of Prosecco. Bright straw yellow in colour with lime green reflections. A smooth effervescence and fine bead. A delicate bouquet on the nose with intense hints of green apple and fresh flowers. Medium sweet and well-structured, pleasant and harmonious on the palate with a dry finish. Ideal as an aperitif and partners well with desserts and nuts.

About Follodor

The Follador Family cares a lot about its cultural heritage in the production of wine. It has neither stood still in the past, nor is it intimidated by modern times, but has quietly married traditional cultivation techniques in the vineyards with the most modern production technologies, this to obtain wines highly regarded even by experts in the sector.
