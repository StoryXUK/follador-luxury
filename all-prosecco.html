Semi-Sparkling Prosecco DOC Treviso

The Latin name of this Follador classic conveys lightness, enjoyable and carefree times. Ideal at any time of the day. With bright straw yellow color and greenish reflections, the richly fruity fragrance is reminiscent of green apple, peach and almond. The taste is smooth and well structured. An excellent aperitif or throughout a meal, it goes well with pasta or rice and light meat dishes.

The native sparkling wines of the territory
Spumanti Millesimati

Historically they represent the native sparkling wines of the Territory. They are mainly produced with grapes from vineyards located in the plains within the Prosecco area. After the cold maceration they are refermented in autoclave for about 60 days, obtaining fresh and fragrant wines, suitable for all convivial occasions.

About Follodor

The Follador Family cares a lot about its cultural heritage in the production of wine. It has neither stood still in the past, nor is it intimidated by modern times, but has quietly married traditional cultivation techniques in the vineyards with the most modern production technologies, this to obtain wines highly regarded even by experts in the sector.
