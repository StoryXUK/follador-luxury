Prosecco DOC Treviso
Prosecco DOC Treviso

These are the sparkling wines that have for time immemorial been native to this land. They are mainly produced with grapes coming from vineyards lying in the lowlands within the Prosecco area. Cold maceration is followed by secondary fermentation in autoclave tanks for approx. 60 days to produce crisp, fragrant wines suitable for all social occasions.

Prosecco DOC Treviso
Semi-Sparkling Prosecco DOC Treviso

The Latin name of this Follador classic conveys lightness, enjoyable and carefree times. Ideal at any time of the day. With bright straw yellow color and greenish reflections, the richly fruity fragrance is reminiscent of green apple, peach and almond. The taste is smooth and well structured. An excellent aperitif or throughout a meal, it goes well with pasta or rice and light meat dishes.

Prosecco DOC Treviso
Rosé Line

A classic Follador wine and a favourite with lovers of its fruity bouquet, typical of rosés. Light pink in color with fine and persistent bubbles. Fruity bouquet, with intense notes of apple, pear and apricot. Recognizable floral scents especially lime blossom. Well-structured to the palate, good balance of acidity and flavor with a clean finish. Excellent aperitif and for cocktails, throughout the meal with dishes based on fish and shellfish.

The native sparkling wines of the territory
Spumanti Millesimati

Historically they represent the native sparkling wines of the Territory. They are mainly produced with grapes from vineyards located in the plains within the Prosecco area. After the cold maceration they are refermented in autoclave for about 60 days, obtaining fresh and fragrant wines, suitable for all convivial occasions.

Follador Prosecco
For All Tastes

Since 1769 when Giovanni Follador received the Wine Quality Award from the Doge of Venice, Alvise IV Mocenigo, the Follador family has humbly followed their passion, setting the standard for Italian wines made with great care and devotion. Gianfranco Follador, Giovanni’s descendant, has been responsible for the winery’s change in direction toward sparkling wines. As one of the original families in the Valdobbiadene area to move into the sparkling arena, Gianfranco, along with his wife Italia, has continued a tradition of quality, passion and wine-making excellence. Follador’s Prosecco lines are the hallmark of great sparkling wines. Offering tiny, persistent bubbles, full-bodied flavor and a fine, dry finish, they are the result of superior wine-making skills that has been passed down for more than 2 centuries.
