Our Prosecco

Valdobbiadene Prosecco Superiore Millesimato D.O.C.G. Brut
Fosélios
Order

The name Fosélios derives from the Greek noun Phos, meaning “Light”, together with the name Helios, the “Sun God”: properties encapsulated within the bright, sunny, “fluorescent” yellow hue of the bottle’s cap. And for an added touch of originality, with an wood light light shining on the label, the Follador logo and the wine’s name “light up”, with a surprising luminous effect that renders any social gathering even more festive and captivating. It’s a fresh, pleasantly sapid spumante.

Organoleptic
Profile

Colour

Fresh and enjoyably flavoursome sparkling wine.

Excellent as an aperitif and with light first courses.
