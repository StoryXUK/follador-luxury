Follador Prosecco Unveils a Sparkling New Online Experience!

We're thrilled to announce the launch of our brand new website and e-commerce store, bringing you an enhanced and seamless way to explore and enjoy Follador Prosecco. As a leading name in the world of Prosecco, we believe that every sip should be an experience, and our new online platform is designed to make that experience even more enjoyable for you.

A Fresh Look and Feel

The first thing you'll notice is our fresh and modern design, reflecting the elegance and sophistication that defines Follador Prosecco. We've carefully curated the visual elements to capture the essence of our brand, ensuring that your visit to our website is not just informative but also visually pleasing.

Easy Navigation for a Effortless Shopping

We understand the importance of a smooth and enjoyable shopping experience. Our new website is designed with user-friendly navigation, making it easier than ever for you to explore our range of Prosecco offerings. Whether you're a connoisseur or a first-time buyer, finding your favourite Follador Prosecco is just a click away.

Exclusive Online Store

Exciting news for our valued customers – we've introduced a dedicated e-commerce store where you can browse, select, and purchase your favourite Follador Prosecco products from the comfort of your home. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or looking for the perfect gift, our online store is your one-stop destination for all things Prosecco.

Special Offers and Promotions

To celebrate the launch of our new online platform, we're offering exclusive promotions and special deals that you won't find anywhere else. Keep an eye out for limited-time offers, bundle deals, and more as we want to share the joy of Follador Prosecco with our online community.

Stay Connected with Follador Prosecco

Our new website is not just a platform for transactions; it's a space for connection. Stay updated with the latest news, events, and Prosecco-related insights through our blog and social media channels. We love engaging with our community, and our online presence is an extension of the warm and welcoming Follador Prosecco family.

Explore, Shop, and Sip with Follador Prosecco

Embark on a journey of taste and elegance with Follador Prosecco's new website and e-commerce store. We invite you to explore, shop, and indulge in the finest Prosecco experience that only Follador can offer.

About Follodor

The Follador Family cares a lot about its cultural heritage in the production of wine. It has neither stood still in the past, nor is it intimidated by modern times, but has quietly married traditional cultivation techniques in the vineyards with the most modern production technologies, this to obtain wines highly regarded even by experts in the sector.
